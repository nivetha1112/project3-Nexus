Prawn Gravy

Ingredients

  • 1kg Fresh Prawn
  • 3 Onion
  • 2 tablespoons Oil
  • Â½ teaspoon Masala
  • 2 Tomato

Instructions

  1. Heat oil in a kadai - add the items under 'to temper' then add onions and garlic fry until it turns slightly brown.Then add ginger garlic paste, fry for a minute. Then add tomato puree along with spice powders and cook till raw smell leaves. Then add marinated prawns.
  2. Saute until prawns starts shrinking say 3mins. Prawn lets out water so check that and then add 1/4 water and keep closed. Cook until prawns is soft and cooked fully. Once the prawns turn soft, add required salt.
  3. Now add the coconut paste and allow it to boil and get cooked until oil separates and the curry becomes saucy. Garnish with chopped coriander leaves and switch it off.
  4. Serve Prawn Gravy hot with rice or rotis as per your choice!
